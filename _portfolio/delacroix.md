## Overview

The **Digital Delacroix** project applies computer vision and digital humanities methods to the full corpus of works by Eugène Delacroix. The goal is to build a structured, searchable digital archive that supports art history research at scale.

The project is funded by the **Schmidt Family Foundation**.
