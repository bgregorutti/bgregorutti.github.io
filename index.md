## About

I am a research engineer and Head of Data Science at [SCAI – Sorbonne Center for Artificial Intelligence](https://scai.sorbonne-universite.fr), where I lead a team of 4 data scientists working on collaborative ML and AI projects with researchers across Sorbonne University's three faculties.

My work spans **computer vision**, **NLP**, **large language models**, and **scientific ML** — applied to domains ranging from art history to molecular chemistry, ecology, and medicine.

I am part of two France 2030-funded programs: **Sorbonne.AI** and **PostGenAI@Paris**.

Before SCAI, I spent 9 years at **Safety Line** (aviation data science) and led technical teams at **LumenAI** (cybersecurity R&D, NLP). I hold a **PhD in Statistics** from Université Pierre et Marie Curie (Paris VI), with a research background in ensemble methods, functional data, and variable selection.
