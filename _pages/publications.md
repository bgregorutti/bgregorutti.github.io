Research output from my time at **Safety Line** (2011–2020) and in collaboration with academic partners (INRIA, LIP6, IFSTTAR). All work is applied to real flight data and aviation problems.

---

## Research Articles

**Block sparse linear models for learning structured dynamical systems in aeronautics**
*Identification of structured dynamical systems from flight data using sparse linear modelling.*

---

**Gaussian Mixture Penalty for Trajectory Optimization Problems**
*Trajectory optimisation under distributional constraints using Gaussian mixture penalties — applied to flight climb profiles.*

---

**Correlation and variable importance in random forests**
*Theoretical and empirical analysis of variable importance measures in random forests in the presence of correlated predictors.*

---

**Grouped variable importance with random forests and application to multivariate functional data analysis**
*Extension of variable importance to grouped variables, with application to multivariate functional data from flight recorders.*

---

**Curve clustering based on second order information: application to bad runway condition detection**
*Clustering of functional data (curves) using second-order statistics — applied to runway condition monitoring from FDR data.*

**Doctor of Philosophy in Statistics**
Université Pierre et Marie Curie (Paris VI) · 2011–2014

Supervised jointly between UPMC and Safety Line.
