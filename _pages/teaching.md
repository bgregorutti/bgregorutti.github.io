## Selected Courses

### Introduction to AI for Non-Technical Audiences
A flagship course developed for students in humanities, medicine, and other non-quantitative fields.

**Format**: no-code workshop + practical Python sessions
**Concepts covered**:
- What is data? What is a model?
- The ML pipeline: training, validation, inference
- Generative AI: how it works, what it can and cannot do
- Hands-on: running and interpreting ML models without writing code
- Practical Python sessions: statistics, visualization, simple ML

This course has been one of the most requested in the SCAI portfolio. The goal is to give non-technical students genuine understanding of AI — not just vocabulary — so they can engage critically with tools they will encounter in their fields.
