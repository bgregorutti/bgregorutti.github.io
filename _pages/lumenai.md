**LumenAI** is a French data science consultancy and R&D company. I joined as **CTO and Head of Data** in November 2020, leading the full technical team through the company's growth phase until May 2022.

---

## Role & Team

- Led a team of **6 people**: data scientists, ML researchers, and data engineers
- Fully **remote management** across three sites: Paris, Rennes, and Pau
- Covered both consulting delivery and internal R&D
- Co-supervised a PhD student · co-author of **1 research article**

## Projects

### Cybersecurity R&D — Network Intrusion Detection
**Partner: DGA (Cyberdefence Factory, Rennes)**

Real-time network intrusion detection using **graph clustering** on live network traffic. The project was funded and supported by the **DGA Cyberdefence Factory** program in Rennes — a French defence agency initiative supporting early-stage cybersecurity R&D.

- Approach: unsupervised, real-time, graph-based anomaly detection
- Delivered a working **prototype** by end of program
- Tech: Python · NetworkX · streaming graph algorithms · real-time data pipelines

---

### Large-Scale Document Indexing — TotalEnergies (Pau)
**Client: TotalEnergies**

Built a system to **index and search a very large internal corpus** of technical text documents for TotalEnergies' datalab in Pau.

- Challenge: heterogeneous document types, large volume, domain-specific vocabulary (geoscience, engineering)
- Approach: NLP-based indexing using **online clustering** for dynamic topic organisation
- Enabled semantic search over the corpus for internal R&D teams
- Tech: Python · NLP · sentence embeddings · online clustering · Elasticsearch

---

### Geoscience Signal Processing — TotalEnergies
**Client: TotalEnergies**

Signal processing and smoothing applied to **geoscience sensor data** collected during field campaigns.

- Applied **computer vision models** (CNN-based) to smooth and denoise irregular time-series and spatial signals from geophysical instruments
- Tech: Python · PyTorch · signal processing · geoscience data formats
