Practical hands-on workshops for researchers and professionals with little or no prior coding experience. Part of the [SCAI Education platform](https://scai-education.sorbonne-universite.fr).

## Topics

- Python basics for researchers
- Data manipulation with pandas
- Visualisation with matplotlib / seaborn
- Introduction to ML with scikit-learn

## Audiences

Medical students, clinicians, researchers in natural sciences, professionals in continuing education.

*First delivered Dec 2022 as part of the initial SCAI Education workshop series; continued through 2023–2026.*
